Step into the World of Organics!

Let the first step be firmly anchored!

The increasing enhancement in modern technology and development in the field of agriculture, is one of the most important and emerging field in the engineering. We are still living in the country where we are following the traditional agricultural methods to grow crops and harvest. We have a numbers of automatic robots and machines which makes the farming process easy and convenient but the problem lies in the financial conditions of the farmers who are unable to afford expensive machines. The idea of this project is to make an efficient website that could help the farmers detect the type of disease, harming the crop, also suggesting the appropriate pesticide. This website can also help farmers to know the weather forecast, so that they can properly plan about harvest.

Crop rotation

  • April 01, 2022
  • !
  • Rohit Kumar

Crop rotation is an efficient way of preventing pests from getting used to the types of plants that are being cultivated. The method uses alternating the species of crops that are grown every year. In addition to managing pests, this farming method also increases the fertility of the soil.

Intercropping

  • April 01, 2022
  • !
  • Mayurakshya

Intercropping involves simultaneous cultivation of two or more crops on the same field. When different species of plants are cultivated on the same field, there is a definite distance between crops of the same species. So, it is efficient in attracting pests away from their target host plant.

Organic Pesticides

  • April 01, 2022
  • !
  • Saurav Kumar

Organic pesticides is how it allows farmers to turn agricultural outputs into natural pesticides and does not affect their health or damage the crops. Farmers in Nepal spray ‘Zhol Mol’ an organic liquid pesticide made of neem leaves, Timur (a Nepali spice), garlic, and water to their vegetables.
